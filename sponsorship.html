Sponsorship Prospectus

Clojure is a growing language that has attracted attention due to its powerful abstractions, well-composed feature set, integration with the JVM and the wealth of available Java libraries.

If you’re interested in interacting with the Clojure community, Clojure/West is an excellent opportunity to reach 400+ Clojure developers. In 2015, Clojure/West had 450 attendees representing small software shops (70%), large companies (20%), and academic institutions (5%). Some companies in attendance in 2014 or 2015 were Apple, Adobe, Amazon, eHarmony, Etsy, Google, Groupon, Heroku, IBM, LinkedIn, Microsoft, Netflix, Oracle, Staples, Thoughtworks, Twitter, and WalmartLabs.

At Clojure/West, we have sponsorship opportunities for you to promote your brand, your product, or your company for hiring. Help us understand your needs so we can make your involvement special.

Please review this prospectus and contact Alex Miller and Lynn Grogan at events@cognitect.com
